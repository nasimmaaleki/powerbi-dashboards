# Retail Sales Dashboard — "Bit"
### Power BI | Business Reporting

An interactive sales dashboard built in Power BI for a retail business, designed to give managers and decision makers a single, clear view of how outlets, products, and locations are performing.

---

## Purpose

The dashboard consolidates the key performance metrics of a retail operation into one interactive page. Rather than reading through raw tables, a manager can see at a glance which outlet types, product categories, and locations drive sales, and where customer satisfaction is strong or weak. It is built to support quick, data driven decisions on stocking, outlet strategy, and product focus.

---

## Dataset

The dashboard is based on a retail outlet sales dataset (the Big Mart sales data), which records product level sales across multiple outlets of different sizes, types, and locations. Each record includes the product, its attributes (such as fat content and item type), the outlet it was sold in, the sales value, and a customer rating.

---

## Key Metrics and Visuals

**Headline KPIs**
- Total Sales (1.20M)
- Average Sales per item (141)
- Number of Items (8,523)
- Average Customer Rating (3.9)

**Breakdowns**
- **Fat content split** — total sales by low fat versus regular products, including a per outlet tier view
- **Item type ranking** — sales contribution of each product category, from fruits and snacks down to seafood
- **Outlet establishment trend** — sales across establishment years (2012 to 2022), highlighting a clear peak in 2018
- **Outlet size** — sales distribution across small, medium, and high size outlets
- **Outlet location tiers** — sales by Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 locations
- **Outlet type table** — a detailed comparison of grocery stores and supermarket types across all metrics, with conditional formatting for quick reading

**Interactivity**
- A filter panel for outlet location type, outlet size, and item type
- A tabbed control to switch the whole view between Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, and Average Rating

---

## Design Approach

The dashboard uses a warm yellow and green theme with a clear visual hierarchy: KPIs at the top for instant context, breakdowns in the middle, and a detailed table at the bottom for those who want the underlying numbers. The tabbed control keeps the layout clean by letting one set of visuals serve four different metrics, rather than crowding the page.
